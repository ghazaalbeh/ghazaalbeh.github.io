1. Paper Clip

Hackarestan: A Hackathon Focusing on Educational Apps

from Jan 27th to 29th 2016 in Tehran

Hackarestan event

Hackarestan is a new series of hackathon events in Iran. The first event have been held from January 27th to 29th in Tehran. The goal of the event was to create a usable software to help the students and the educational community in Iran. I participated in this event. I was a member of "I4" group. We created an app which helps teachers to more easily teach concepts of the course. Our app name was "Paper Clip".

Paper Clip Application

Imagine a teacher who saw a picture in a textbook and he decided to give the students additional information in the form of video ,audio, photo or pdf. He can use "Paper Clip". This project consists of two parts: 1. An admin panel for teachers to upload their informations about pictures in the text book. 2. An android application for students to download the information. It is necessary to mention, Students just have to capture the image , and then receive information.

My role in the project

I have created an admin panel for teachers. I created this admin panel with PHP & MySQL. In addition, My other task was designing and implementing the user interface of android application.
